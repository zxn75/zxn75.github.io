Mario

The game Super Mario Bros. had been around for decades, going all the way back to the year 1983. Since then the game has had many different versions as nintedo puts out more of its consoles. The character Mario is not the only iconic character in his games. Some others may include but are not limited to Luigi, Bowser, Bowser Jr. along with the Koopa kids, Peach, Yoshi and many more, some of these characters even have their own games. Some of his more recent game include Super Mario Odyssey, Mario Karts, and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate.
